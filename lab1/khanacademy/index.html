Whispers of the Unseen

In the stillness,
where shadows dance,
Quiet secrets find their chance—
A whispered breeze,
a fleeting sigh,
A language that we can't deny.
The moonlight hums a gentle tune
Beneath the vast and silent moon,
Its glow, a pulse, a tender beam,
Unveiling all we cannot deem.
What’s felt, but never quite displayed,
Lives in the spaces where light fades.
A truth that only hearts can know,
A current hidden, soft as snow.
